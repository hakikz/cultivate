Progressive Design to Transform Apartment Living

Set at an elevated point, at the gateway to Melbourne’s premium northern neighbourhoods, Cultivate is a considered response to the rapid evolution of Heidelberg West. With the most breathtaking city views and expansive outdoor realms, an exclusive lifestyle collaboration from leading Australian designers has been carefully composed for modern living.

The dual tower, eleven level residential landmark progresses an emerging urban destination with architecture that borrows from its environment using naturally inspired materials and textures and an abundance of light and unexpected luxury. With an exclusive selection of 1, 2 & 3 bedroom residences feature spacious floorplans, full size kitchens and premium finishes throughout to herald a new era for apartment living.

LOCATION AND LIFESTYLE

The green heart of Melbourne offers natural river-ways and wide, leafy green spaces without sacrificing inner city amenity. Retail establishments, cafes and restaurants prove themselves as a convenient trait of the region’s flourishing lifestyle.

Places to play, row, ride, relax and explore help encourage locals to slow the pace and find time to breathe. Local tracks and trails invite a morning jog or a leisurely stroll along the Yarra River banks or under the cooling canopies of Darebin Creek’s Forest Park.
